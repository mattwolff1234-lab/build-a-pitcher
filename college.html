How to Play

Pick a position - QB, RB, or WR - then build the ultimate college star, one body part at a time.

  1. Spin the reel. It stops on a random College Football 27 player.
  2. Assign that player to one of the 9 body parts. Each part is an attribute - a QB's Arm Power, a RB's Break Tackle, a WR's Hands - and the slot takes that player's rating for it.
  3. Fill every part to finish your player and reveal their Overall rating. Card colors show how strong a player is, from grey up to diamond, with purple for retired college legends.
  4. Simulate College Career: Signing Day, season by season, Heisman races, the Playoff - then your NFL Draft projection.
  5. Name your player and post them to the leaderboard.

Power-ups

You get one of each per run, so spend them wisely.

  • Re-spin: toss the current player and spin for a new one.
  • Boost: upgrade the current player to their Prime version with stronger ratings, then assign them.
  • Snag: grab the player one spot to the left or right of where you landed.
📊 What matters most

Your Overall is a weighted average · each position has three premium slots (1.2×):

QBShort, Mid & Deep Accuracy count extra · Arm Power, Poise, Football IQ are strong seconds.
RBSpeed, Break Tackle & Vision count extra · Burst, Elusiveness, Power are strong seconds.
WRHands, Speed & Routes count extra · Release, In Traffic, Spectacular are strong seconds.
1.0×Frame · pure height, no cap benefit - a safe spot for a weaker card.
