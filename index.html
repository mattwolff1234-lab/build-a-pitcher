📖 How to Play
On Desktop / Android (Chrome · Edge)
1
Look for the install icon

Click the install icon (a screen with a ⊕) on the right side of the address bar.

2
Click Install

Confirm in the prompt - the app opens in its own window with a home-screen icon.

On iPhone / iPad (Safari)
1
Open in Safari

Make sure you're in Safari - not Chrome or another browser.

2
Tap Share

Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up).

3
Add to Home Screen

Scroll down, tap "Add to Home Screen," then tap "Add."
