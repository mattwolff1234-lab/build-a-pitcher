## 01 · What GoatLab is

GoatLab is a family of **free** browser games. You spin a slot-machine reel of **real
athletes**, build a player one body part at a time, then **simulate their entire
career** — awards, records, a Hall of Fame verdict. Then climb global leaderboards, face
other players **1v1 live**, and survive a roguelike **dream-team gauntlet**.

No download. No install. It plays in any phone or laptop browser. → **goat-lab.app**

**Why it makes great content**
- **Fast payoff.** Spin → reveal → Overall → full career, all in under two minutes. Built for short-form pacing.
- **RNG does the work.** Pulling a purple **Legend** or a 99 is a real on-camera reaction.
- **Decisions your audience argues about.** Boost it? Snag the neighbor? Burn the re-spin?
- **One life.** The GOAT Squad Gauntlet turns every run into a story with a win-or-die ending.
- **Play your people.** 1v1 Face Off lets you take on your own viewers, live.
- **Built to be posted.** Every run ends in a screenshot-ready career card *and* a Wordle-style emoji grid.

## 04 · ★ GOAT Squad — the flagship (please run the Gauntlet)

GOAT Squad is a different game entirely. The reels **never stop on their own** — you
**tap STOP and lock** the player you want into your lineup. It's a real skill. Then your
squad goes to battle.

**Play the GAUNTLET and get as far as you possibly can.** It's a roguelike, and it's the
best content on the whole site:

- A **10-stage map** — eight fights and two shops, with **forks**: the safe road, or the harder one with better spoils.
- You draft your squad **once**, at fight one. It rides with you the entire run.
- **ONE life.** Lose a series and the run is **over** — no restarts, no refresh-scumming.
- Beat a team → spin their roster on a **spoils wheel** and sign a player. Collect **relics** (max 5) that bend the rules. Stack **banners** for tougher fights and richer rewards.
- Reach **8 / 8** and you're a 👑 **Champion** on the leaderboard.

**Why it's elite content:** it's a natural episode. Tension every fight, meta decisions
your chat will backseat, and a clean win-or-die ending. *"How far can we get with ONE
life?"* is a whole video — or a whole stream.

→ **goat-lab.app/goatsquad** (also **squad.goat-lab.app**). Baseball & football editions:
`/squad-baseball` · `/squad-football`.

## 05 · ⚔️ 1v1 Face Off — play your viewers

Real-time online PvP against another human. You match up; one of you builds the pitcher,
the other the batter (or hoops / soccer / CFB) under a shot clock. **Higher Overall
wins.** On a livestream, this is the segment that turns a viewer into a player.

**Three ways to play your audience live:**
- **Friendly challenge (most reliable).** You and the viewer add each other by **@handle** (☰ → 👥 Friends → search), then send a challenge — you drop into a private lobby together. No rank at risk.
- **Open queue.** You both hit goat-lab.app/versus and search at the same time to get paired into the live pool.
- **King of the hill.** Winner stays on; chat lines up to dethrone you. Empty lobby? Hit **"Face a Ghost"** — a real recent build — to keep the segment moving.

→ `/versus` · `/versus-hoops` · `/versus-soccer` · `/versus-cfb`

## 07 · Platform playbooks

### 📱 TikTok + IG Reels — `9:16 · 15–45s`

Vertical, trending or high-energy audio, big burned-in captions, fast cuts. One idea, one payoff.

**Beat sheet:** `0–2s` Hook — state the stakes → **Build** one or two spins + a big reveal →
**Payoff** the Overall / the HOF verdict / the win → `≤3s` CTA — link & handle.

**Hooks that work:**
- "This slot machine decides a Hall of Fame career."
- "I have *one life* to build a team that beats the '27 Yankees."
- "Comment a body part and I'll put the next player there."
- "Building the most broken pitcher the game will let me."

**Land on a payoff:** a purple Legend pull, a 99+ Overall, a first-ballot HOF, a 👑 Gauntlet
champ, a walk-off 1v1 win. **CTA line:** *"Free on goat-lab.app — link in bio. Add me @handle."*

- ✅ **Do:** hook in the first second; one clear payoff; keep it under 45s.
- ❌ **Don't:** explain the rules before the first spin, or bury the reveal.

### 𝕏 (X) — video + posts — `native · 30–90s`

**Upload video directly** (X favors native and autoplays muted — **burn in captions**). But X
is also a **text** game, and it's where the shareable grid lives:
- **Post your Daily grid every day** — the 🐐 emoji block. It's spoiler-safe and needs no link; the signature line *is* the hook.
- **"Beat my Overall"** posts with your career-card screenshot.
- **Quote-tweet your followers'** grids and builds; run a daily "best build wins."
- **Challenge your followers to a 1v1** — drop your @handle and tell them to add you at goat-lab.app.

- ✅ **Do:** lead with the card or grid image; one clear ask; keep the grid spoiler-safe.
- ❌ **Don't:** bury the video mid-thread, or post player names alongside a Daily grid.

### ▶️ YouTube — long-form + Shorts — `8–20 min`

Long-form is where GoatLab shines — the careers and the Gauntlet are actual narratives.
- **"I simulated [player]'s entire career"** in this slot-machine game.
- **A full GOAT Squad Gauntlet run** — one life, start to finish. Natural act structure.
- **"Building the most broken [position] possible"** — or a tier list.
- **"I let my chat build an NFL QB"** — the college → go-pro arc.
- **1v1 vs subscribers.**

**Structure:** cold-open your best moment (0–15s) → a quick *"here's the game, logging in so
it saves"* → the run → the payoff → an end screen with the link, your @handle, and a "play
today's Daily, comment your grid" prompt. Repurpose your TikToks as Shorts.

- ✅ **Do:** cold-open the payoff; pin a comment with the link + @handle + Daily prompt.
- ❌ **Don't:** spend three minutes on rules before the first spin.

### 🔴 Livestream — `Twitch · YouTube · Kick`

GoatLab is genuinely built for streams, because two segments are interactive — lean on them:
- **GOAT Squad Gauntlet — the marathon.** One life; chat backseats every STOP and every lock. *"How far can we get?"* carries a whole stream, and relics / banners / insurance give you meta calls chat will fight about.
- **1v1 Face Off vs viewers — the main event.** Add viewers by @handle → friendly challenge, or queue together. King of the hill: winner stays on.
- **Daily race vs chat** — everyone builds the same seeded Daily; best Overall wins.

**Overlay:** keep your **@handle** and **goat-lab.app** on screen the whole stream so
drop-ins can add and follow you.

- ✅ **Do:** sign in **first** (viewer challenges + saves need it); let chat make the calls.
- ❌ **Don't:** forget to claim your handle before you go live.
