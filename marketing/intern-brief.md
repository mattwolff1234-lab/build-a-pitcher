# GoatLab Creator Outreach: the gig (for Javin)

Everything for the creator outreach gig in one place.

## What it is
You'll be reaching out to creators to get them to play GoatLab and post about it. We've got
games for **all the major sports** (baseball, basketball, soccer, college football), so
**sports creators across any sport are fair game**, and **gaming creators in general are a
secondary target.** You'd be reaching out through **X and email primarily.** The counseling
job shouldn't be an issue since this is all evening/async stuff, and honestly evenings are
better for creator replies anyway.

## Platforms we want them posting on
**X, TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Twitch.** Any of those is a win. I care most about
**YouTube and Twitch** since they convert the best and we can actually track them.

## Who to hit up
- I'll send you a **list of ~55 creators** with priorities on it, so start at the top and
  work down.
- Also **source your own.** At least **5k followers**, posts about sports (any sport works,
  gaming as a secondary), active in the last couple weeks, real engagement (actual comments,
  not bots). A small account that replies beats a huge one that ghosts.
- Every new one you find goes in the sheet: handle, follower count, one line on why they
  fit.

## How to reach out
- I'll give you the **scripts/templates.** Use them, but always tweak the first line so
  it's personal, like mentioning one of their recent posts or videos. No lazy copy-paste
  blasts, that's the whole game here.
- **X DMs and email are the two channels.** YouTubers/streamers usually list a business
  email on their About page, so email those. Everyone else, DM on X.
- Max ~10-15 reach-outs a day per platform. Only follow up **once** if they ghost (like
  5-7 days later), then move on.

## Tracking
Every creator gets their own **tracking link** so we can see exactly how many new players
they send us. For **YouTube especially**, the creator drops the link in their description
so we can count how many new users come from that video. That's how we know who's actually
driving people vs. just posting.

Important: a post only counts once it's **live and has the link in it.** A shoutout with no
link doesn't count, since we can't track it and it barely sends anyone anyway.

## Pay
- **$50/week for the first two weeks**, a trial run for both of us.
- Bonuses on top of that:
  - **$10** every time someone you reached out to actually posts a video/post
  - **$25** for a livestream
  - **$10 for every 1,000 new players** your creators send us (each person counted once, not
    raw plays), tracked through the links (mostly YouTube, X, and Twitch)
  - All bonuses **capped at $500** total.
- Paid **every Friday** (Venmo/Zelle, whatever's easiest for you).
- If it's clicking after the two weeks, we keep rolling and I'll bump up the base.

## Couple ground rules
- **Never** say we're affiliated with MLB, MLB The Show, or EA. It's "real MLB ratings,"
  that's it.
- If anyone asks, just be upfront you're reaching out on behalf of GoatLab.
- Keep it human in the DMs, you're a real person who thinks the game's cool, not a bot.

## Logging
Everything goes in the shared sheet: who you contacted, date, platform, did they reply, did
they post, and their tracking link. That sheet is how we settle up the bonuses each Friday,
so keep it current.
