## Sending rules

- 10–15 DMs per day per platform max (spam filters + it keeps personalization honest).
- Track every send in `creator-targets.md` (date, template used, reply, next step).
- One follow-up, ever. Silence after that = move on; re-approach next season with new hook.
- When a creator posts organically: reply/boost from the brand account same day, DM a thank
  you + offer a paid repeat. Organic posters convert to the best long-term paid partners.
