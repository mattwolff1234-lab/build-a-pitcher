About GOAT Squad Football

GOAT Squad Football is a free dream-team builder played on a gridiron. Every position on the field spins at once and you hit STOP to freeze the board, then lock your eleven one player at a time — quarterback, skill positions, the line, defence and a head coach.

Then the Gauntlet tests the roster against a ladder of the greatest teams ever assembled, with cap space to spend between rounds, relics that change the rules, and a single life for the whole run.

A new daily squad drops every day with the same reels for every player worldwide.

Frequently asked questions

Is GoatLab free to play?

Yes. Every game runs in your browser with no download and no payment. An optional account saves your builds, and optional extras exist, but the games themselves are free.

Do I need an account?

No. You can play, post scores and take on the daily challenge as a guest. Signing in with Google just saves your players to a personal Hall of Fame and carries your progress between devices.

How is this different from a fantasy draft?

You never pick freely from a full pool. The reels decide what is available at the instant you stop them, so timing and knowing when to settle are the real skills.

What happens if I lose?

The run ends. There is one life per gauntlet, so every series matters — though shops sell insurance that can save you once.
