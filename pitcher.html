How to Play

Build the ultimate pitcher, one body part at a time.

  1. Spin the reel. It stops on a random pitcher.
  2. Assign that pitcher to one of the 9 body parts. Each part is an attribute like Velocity, Command, or Strikeout, and the slot takes that pitcher's rating for it.
  3. Fill all 9 parts to finish your pitcher and reveal their Overall rating. Card colors show how strong a pitcher is, from grey up to diamond, with purple for legends.
  4. Simulate Career to play out their seasons and see if they reach the Hall of Fame.
  5. Name your pitcher and post them to the leaderboard.

Power-ups

You get one of each per run, so spend them wisely.

  • Re-spin: toss the current pitcher and spin for a new one.
  • Boost: upgrade the current pitcher to their Prime version with stronger ratings, then assign them.
  • Snag: grab the pitcher one spot to the left or right of where you landed.

Tip: your strongest pitchers do the most good on Strikeout, Command, and Ground Ball.

📊 What matters most

Your Overall is a weighted average · these slots count extra, best first:

1.2×Strikeout, Ground Ball, Command · the three "true outcomes" (strikeouts, homers allowed, walks). Park your best ratings here.
1.1×Velocity, Break, Stamina, Clutch · strong seconds.
0.4–1.3×Defense & Frame · these barely count when they're average, but an elite glove (85+) or a 6'6"+ frame ramps up to full weight and becomes a real edge.

Stamina also drives innings and career length in the sim · monster strikeout careers need Strikeout and Stamina.
