How to Play

Build the ultimate baller, one body part at a time.

  1. Spin the reel. It stops on a random player.
  2. Assign that player to one of the 9 body parts. Each part is an attribute like 3-Pointer, Finishing, Defense, or Dribble, and the slot takes that player's rating for it.
  3. Fill every part to finish your hooper and reveal their Overall rating. Card colors show how strong a hooper is, from grey up to diamond, with purple for legends.
  4. Simulate Career to play out their seasons and see if they reach the Hall of Fame.
  5. Name your hooper and post them to the leaderboard.

Power-ups

You get one of each per run, so spend them wisely.

  • Re-spin: toss the current player and spin for a new one.
  • Boost: upgrade the current player to their Prime version with stronger ratings, then assign them.
  • Snag: grab the player one spot to the left or right of where you landed.

Tip: your strongest players do the most good on 3-Pointer, Finishing, and Playmaking.
